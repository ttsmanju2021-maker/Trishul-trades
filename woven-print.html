    What are Woven Labels?

    Woven labels are high-quality textile labels created by weaving threads together to form text and logos directly into the fabric. They are widely used in garments, accessories, and fashion products for branding and care information.

    Compared to printed labels, woven labels are more durable, premium in appearance, and resistant to washing, heat, and daily wear. They are ideal for clothing brands looking for a long-lasting and professional finish.
